Puppy

Puppies are cute and full of energy. Like human babies, puppies need lots of attention and supervision. Puppies will need to be trained from scratch and socialized properly. If you have a lot of time to devote, then a puppy might be right for you!

Young

Young dogs still have bundles of energy in them and need constant exercise. While most young dogs have basic training, it is your job to continue the training and fix some bad habits the dog picked up as a puppy. A young dog is great for someone who wants to see a dog mature emotionally and physically.

Adult

First time dog owners should consider adopting an adult dog. Adult dogs are physically and emotionally matured. You know what you are getting when adopting an adult dog. Adult dogs require less supervision than younger dogs and will be very loyal towards you.

Senior

If you want a sweet loving dog that will be by your side constantly, a senior dog will be perfect. Senior dogs are gentle, and will thank you for making their last years their best years Often overlooked at shelters for younger dogs, many older dogs don't get the chance to find a loving family even though they fit many living arrangements.
